Why this feels “Awesome”

Connection is infrastructure. Westwood has students, renters, events, meetings, coffee shops, civic life, arts, and public programs — but people need a simple way to see them at the exact moment they are deciding whether to stay in or show up.

A small grant can make the prototype feel real enough to test with student groups, renters, community leaders, local businesses, and neighborhood organizations.
$1,000 grant use

What the Awesome Foundation grant unlocks.

The $1,000 would turn this from a polished concept into a more usable public prototype: faster mobile design, cleaner event cards, map improvements, basic analytics, hosting/domain costs, flyer cleanup, accessibility fixes, and a small outreach push to student groups, renters, neighborhood leaders, and local organizations.
